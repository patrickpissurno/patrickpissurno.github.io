Slide Remote

Slide Remote is an app that let's you easily remote control your presentations

Designed by Freepik

Why is it so amazing?

You can control any presentation software you want: Prezi, Microsoft PowerPoint, Slideshare and many more!

And the best:
- No registration required!
- No ads!
- Plug and play Windows app!
- Unlimited usage!
- High performace!
- Works with proxies without any additional effort!
- Control multiple computers with a single device!
- Pretty looking material design interface!
