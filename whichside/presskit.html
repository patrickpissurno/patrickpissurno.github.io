Description

Which Side is an action arcade game where your motor coordination skills are tested to the limit. You hear a beep in your left, right or both ears and have to guess from Which Side it came to press the right button. The goal is to score the maximum amount of points without making any mistake.

History

Which Side idea came up when a friend of mine threw me a bottle of water shouting “Patrick, Catch it!” while I was distracted. I had to turn fast enough to catch the bottle, but I couldn’t. Then, I tought to myself: “Wow! Wouldn’t it be amazing if I had a game to improve my reflex skills?”. Picked up my school notebook from my backpack and started to draft. One year later, after learning Art, Design, some strange coding stuff (a lot of coffee and sleepless nights), the dream became reality.
My full name is Patrick Motta Aragão Pissurno, I’m from Brazil and I’m 17. I study at NAVE (a technical school where we learn how to code and make games) and I really like to create stuff. From games and apps to the back-end development. I like to think of myself as an one-man army. Not because I’m good at every part of a project, but because I like to do every part of them (except fixing bugs… So boring xD). I did every single piece of Which Side and now I think of it as a baby. From the sketchs to the store. I’m very happy.
